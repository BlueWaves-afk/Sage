## Profile
[[KazMunayGas]] is the national oil and gas company of Kazakhstan. It plays a pivotal role in the country's energy sector, managing significant upstream, midstream, and downstream operations. The company is instrumental in Kazakhstan's oil production and export, contributing substantially to the nation's economy.

## Role
[[KazMunayGas]] is a key supplier in the global oil market, with a daily export capacity of 1.4 million barrels per day (mbpd). Its operations are crucial for maintaining Kazakhstan's position as a significant oil exporter. The company utilizes various export routes, including pipelines and the [[Suez Canal]], to deliver oil to international markets.

## Risk
As a major state-owned enterprise, [[KazMunayGas]] is subject to geopolitical risks associated with Kazakhstan's strategic position and relations with neighboring countries. Additionally, the company faces operational risks related to infrastructure dependencies, such as the [[Suez Canal]], which can impact global supply chains.

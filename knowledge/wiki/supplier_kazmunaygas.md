## Profile
[[KazMunayGas]] is the national oil and gas company of Kazakhstan. It plays a pivotal role in the country's energy sector, managing significant upstream, midstream, and downstream operations. The company is instrumental in Kazakhstan's oil production and export, contributing substantially to the nation's economy.

## Role
[[KazMunayGas]] is responsible for approximately 1.4 million barrels per day (mbpd) of oil exports. It operates both domestically and internationally, with a focus on expanding its global footprint. The company utilizes various export routes, including pipelines and the [[Suez Canal]], to deliver oil to global markets.

## Risk
As a major player in Kazakhstan's oil sector, [[KazMunayGas]] faces risks associated with geopolitical tensions, market volatility, and operational challenges. However, it is not currently subject to international sanctions, which allows it to maintain its export levels and engage in global trade without significant restrictions.

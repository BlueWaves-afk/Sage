## Profile
The [[Panipat]], located at coordinates 29.39°N latitude and 76.97°E longitude, is a significant asset in India's oil refining capacity. It processes approximately 0.30 million barrels per day (mbpd) and maintains an inventory sufficient for 22 days of operation.

## Role
The [[Panipat]] plays a critical role in India's domestic oil supply chain by converting crude oil into refined petroleum products. It contributes to meeting the regional demand for fuels and petrochemicals, supporting both local consumption and distribution networks.

## Risk
Operational risks at the [[Panipat]] include supply chain vulnerabilities, particularly concerning the consistent availability of crude oil. Fluctuations in the supply of grades like [[Arab Light]] could impact refinery operations and product output. Additionally, geopolitical tensions in oil-producing regions may affect the stability of crude oil imports.

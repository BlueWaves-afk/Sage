## Profile
The Panipat Refinery, located at coordinates 29.39°N latitude and 76.97°E longitude, is a significant asset in India's oil refining landscape. It has a processing capacity of 0.30 million barrels per day (mbpd). The refinery maintains an inventory sufficient for 22 days of operation, ensuring a buffer against supply chain disruptions.

## Role
Panipat Refinery plays a critical role in India's energy security by converting crude oil into refined petroleum products. Its strategic location allows it to serve a vast consumer base, contributing to the stability of the domestic fuel market. The refinery processes various crude types, including [[Arab Light]], optimizing its operational flexibility and supply chain resilience.

## Risk
Operational risks at Panipat Refinery include potential supply chain interruptions, given its dependency on imported crude oil such as [[Arab Light]]. Geopolitical tensions in oil-producing regions could impact crude availability and pricing. Additionally, the 22-day inventory buffer, while substantial, may be tested during prolonged disruptions, affecting the refinery's output and the downstream fuel supply.

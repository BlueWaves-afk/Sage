## Profile
[[NIOC]] (National Iranian Oil Company) historically supplied Iranian Heavy to Indian refiners,
notably [[Mangaluru]]. Indian imports are effectively zero since 2019 under comprehensive US and
EU sanctions.

## Sanctions Status
SDN-listed by [[OFAC]] with secondary-sanctions risk for any purchaser; under [[EU]] oil-sector
sanctions. Any resumption requires an OFAC waiver — none active.

## SAGE Risk Profile
Critical sanctions risk; excluded from the available-supplier set. Iranian escalation around the
[[Strait of Hormuz]] is, however, a leading geopolitical risk driver for the corridor itself.

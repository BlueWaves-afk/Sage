## Profile
The [[Chennai Refinery]], located at coordinates 13.16 latitude and 80.26 longitude, has a processing capacity of 0.21 million barrels per day (mbpd). It maintains an inventory sufficient for 22 days of operation.

## Role
The refinery plays a critical role in processing crude oil into refined petroleum products for local and regional consumption. Its strategic location enables it to serve as a key node in the Indian Oil Corporation's supply chain network, contributing to energy security in the region.

## Risk
Operational risks include potential supply chain disruptions, particularly concerning crude oil imports from regions like [[Cabinda]], which could impact the refinery's throughput and inventory levels. Geopolitical tensions and market volatility in crude oil supply regions pose additional risks to the refinery's stable operation.

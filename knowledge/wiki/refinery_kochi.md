## Profile
The [[Kochi]], situated at coordinates 9.97°N latitude and 76.28°E longitude, has a processing capacity of 0.31 million barrels per day (mbpd). It maintains an inventory sufficient for 22 days of operation.

## Role
The [[Kochi]] processes a variety of crude oils, including [[Qatar Marine]] and [[Bonny Light]], contributing to the regional supply of refined petroleum products.

## Risk
Operational risks include potential supply chain disruptions due to geopolitical tensions affecting crude oil imports, particularly for [[Qatar Marine]] and [[Bonny Light]]. Additionally, the refinery's proximity to coastal areas poses environmental risks from maritime incidents.

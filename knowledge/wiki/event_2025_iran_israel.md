## Event Overview
In June 2025, Iran and Israel engaged in direct military strikes, significantly raising the risk of closure of the [[Strait of Hormuz]]. The conflict severity was assessed at 0.9.

## Context
The 2025 Iran-Israel Conflict is part of an ongoing geopolitical and military confrontation between the two nations, which transitioned from proxy hostilities to direct clashes in 2024.

## Impact on Oil Supply Chain
The direct military strikes between Iran and Israel in June 2025 posed a critical threat to the stability of the oil supply chain, particularly through the Strait of Hormuz, a vital chokepoint for global oil shipments.

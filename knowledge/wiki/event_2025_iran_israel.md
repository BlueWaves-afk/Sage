## GeoEvent Summary
In June 2025, Iran and Israel engaged in direct military strikes, significantly raising the risk of closure of the [[Strait of Hormuz]]. The conflict severity was assessed at 0.9 on a scale where 1.0 indicates maximum severity.

## Conflict Overview
The 2025 Iran-Israel Conflict, part of the ongoing Iran–Israel conflict, saw direct military engagements between the two nations. This phase of the conflict followed years of proxy hostilities and escalated into direct confrontations starting in 2024.

## Strait of Hormuz Impact
The military strikes between Iran and Israel in June 2025 heightened the risk of the [[Strait of Hormuz]] being closed. The Strait is a critical chokepoint for global oil supplies, and its closure would have severe implications for global energy security.

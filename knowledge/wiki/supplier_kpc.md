## Overview
[[Kuwait Petroleum Corporation]] (KPC) is Kuwait's national state-owned oil company, headquartered in Kuwait City. It is involved in petroleum exploration, production, refining, marketing, and transportation. KPC is a significant player in the global oil market, producing about 7% of the world's total crude oil.

## Key Facts
- **Country**: Kuwait
- **Daily Export**: 2.0 million barrels per day (mbpd)
- **Sanctioned**: False

## Recent Developments
- In January 2025, KPC discovered an estimated 800 million barrels of medium-density oil and 600 billion standard cubic feet of associated gas at the Al-Jlaiaa offshore field.
- In March 2026, due to regional conflicts, KPC reduced oil output and temporarily suspended production at the Mina Abdullah and Mina Al-Ahmadi refineries following drone attacks.

## Geopolitical Considerations
- KPC's operations are influenced by regional stability, particularly concerning the [[Strait of Hormuz]], a critical chokepoint for global oil shipments.

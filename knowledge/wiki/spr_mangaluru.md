## Profile
The SPRCavern "Mangaluru SPR" is a strategic petroleum reserve facility located in [[Mangaluru]], Karnataka. It is designed with a storage capacity of 1.50 million metric tons (mmt) of crude oil. 

## Role
The primary role of the Mangaluru SPR is to ensure energy security by storing crude oil, which can be released in the event of supply disruptions. It serves as a buffer to stabilize domestic oil supply and prices.

## Risk
The facility faces risks associated with natural disasters, such as cyclones and flooding, given its coastal location. Additionally, operational risks include potential leaks or technical failures that could impact the stored reserves.

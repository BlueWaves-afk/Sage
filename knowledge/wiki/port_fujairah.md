The port is part of the [[Fujairah]] emirate, known for its strategic location outside the [[Strait of Hormuz]], providing a secure alternative for oil shipments.

Fujairah plays a pivotal role in the regional oil supply chain, particularly for [[ADNOC]], by offering robust storage and bunkering services. ## Risk

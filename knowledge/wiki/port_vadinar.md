## Overview
[[Vadinar]] is a deep-water crude terminal on the Gulf of Kutch and the primary import gateway for
the [[Jamnagar Refinery]]. Single-buoy moorings handle VLCCs up to ~315,000 DWT at 23 m draft.

## Role in the Chain
All Gulf crude — from [[Saudi Aramco]], [[ADNOC]], and the [[Iraqi Oil Ministry]] — transits the
[[Strait of Hormuz]] before discharging at Vadinar, which then SUPPLIES the [[Jamnagar Refinery]].

## SAGE Risk Profile
Direct Hormuz linkage. [[Yanbu]]-routed crude can reach Vadinar via the Red Sea and Arabian Sea
without a port change, making it the natural discharge point for the bypass scenario.

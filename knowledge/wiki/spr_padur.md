## Profile
The SPRCavern "Padur SPR" is a strategic petroleum reserve facility located in Padur, Udupi, Karnataka. It has a storage capacity of 2.50 million metric tons (mmt) of crude oil.

## Role
The Padur SPR serves as a critical component in India's energy security strategy. It is designed to store crude oil to be used in the event of supply disruptions, thereby ensuring a stable energy supply for the nation.

## Risk
The primary risks associated with the Padur SPR include geopolitical tensions that could lead to supply chain disruptions, natural disasters affecting the facility's operations, and the potential for cyber-attacks on its infrastructure.

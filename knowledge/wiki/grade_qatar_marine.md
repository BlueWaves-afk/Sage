## Profile
[[Qatar Marine]] is a medium-light crude oil grade originating from [[QatarEnergy]]. It has an API gravity of 36.0 and a sulfur content of 1.40%, classifying it as a low-sulfur crude. 

## Role
The primary role of [[Qatar Marine]] in the oil supply chain is as a feedstock for refineries, particularly those in Asia. Its product yield includes 24% naphtha, 14% kerosene, 21% diesel, 21% vacuum gas oil (VGO), and 20% residue. These yields make it suitable for producing a range of refined products, catering to diverse market demands.

## Risk
Geopolitical stability in [[QatarEnergy]] and the broader Persian Gulf region directly impacts the supply of [[Qatar Marine]]. Any disruptions in this region could affect the crude's availability and pricing. Additionally, refining complexities due to its specific yield profile may pose operational risks for refineries not optimized for such crude characteristics.

## Overview
[[Vizag SPR]] is India's first commissioned strategic petroleum reserve (ISPRL), an underground
rock cavern at Visakhapatnam with 1.33 MMT capacity.

## Role in the Chain
Part of India's 5.33 MMT reserve alongside [[Mangaluru SPR]] and [[Padur SPR]] — together ~9.5 days
of import cover. Drawn down to bridge a feedstock gap at refineries such as the
[[Jamnagar Refinery]] during a [[Strait of Hormuz]] disruption.

## Draw Policy
Emergency drawdown is staged by System 4's SDP/CMDP solver; typical max rate ≈ 0.18 MT/day while
maintaining the buffer floor. Current fill is estimated at ~40% of capacity.

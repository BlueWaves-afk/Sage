## Profile
[[Saudi Aramco]] is the world's largest oil producer and one of India's largest crude suppliers.
It ships primarily via the [[Strait of Hormuz]] to [[Vadinar]] and [[Sikka]], feeding the
[[Jamnagar Refinery]].

## Export Grades
Aramco's slate spans [[Arab Extra Light]], [[Arab Light]], [[Arab Medium]], and [[Arab Heavy]] —
medium-sour grades well matched to [[Jamnagar Refinery]]'s high-complexity configuration.

## Bypass Capability
If the [[Strait of Hormuz]] closes, Aramco can shift volume through the East–West (Petroline)
pipeline to [[Yanbu]] on the Red Sea — the primary BYPASS_ROUTE in SAGE, adding ~7–12 days transit
and a ~$2.50/bbl freight premium.

## Current Status
Active exporter, no sanctions. The lowest-risk anchor supplier in the procurement set.
